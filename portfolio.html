ABOUT
Process, not just polish

I'm a designer who never fully chose a lane — and stopped apologizing for it.

My background started in print production: registration marks, spot colors, press checks. That discipline — everything measured, everything accountable to a physical output — still shapes how I approach a Figma file or a 300×250 banner today.

Clients bring me in when a project needs to move between mediums: a product's UI needs to feel like its packaging, a campaign needs to work identically as a billboard and a mobile banner. I build the shared system underneath so nothing feels like a translation.

Based in Kochi, working with teams everywhere.
